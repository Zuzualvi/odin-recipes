Shrimp Cocktail Recipe

Description

This is an easy shrimp cocktail recipe that can be prepared on gameday. Impress all of your friends during the game by bringing out this classic gameday snack.

Who needs chips when football can be accompanied by shrimp cocktail

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Ketchup
  • Lemon
  • Horseradish
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Mix ketchup and horseradish together
  2. Add hot sauce and mix
  3. Garnish with lemon
  4. Line shrimp around the bowl
