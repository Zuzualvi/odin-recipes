Brisket Recipe

Description

This suculent brisket is a perfect recipe to make for the holidays! Impress the entire family with this easy set it and forget it recipe.

Please note, this recipe does contain meat so it is not for vegetarians. Dont be stupid and serve this to vegans either. The meat lovers, however, will love this recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef Brisket Cut
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Smoke Brisket
  2. Rub brown sugar
  3. Rub garlic powder
  4. Add BBQ Sauce
