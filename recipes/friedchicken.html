Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

An amazing fried chicken recipe that can be made in under an hour. Enjoy this for a simple meal everyone will lovers at family dinner.

This chicken recipe makes well seasoned chicken and can be paired with with any sauce. Feel free to go buy some or even make your own to see for yourself

Ingredients

  • Chicken Strips
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Cayenne
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix baking soda, flour, water, cayenne, and salt in a bowl
  2. Dip chicken strips into batter
  3. Fry chicken in deep fryer or air fryer
